Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse through our product categories, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You’ll need to provide your delivery information and confirm payment via M-Pesa.

2. Can I pay using M-Pesa?

Yes, we accept payments through M-Pesa. During checkout, select the M-Pesa payment option, enter your phone number, and follow the instructions to complete your payment securely.

3. How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary depending on your location. Most orders are delivered within 24-48 hours. For remote areas, delivery may take up to 72 hours.

4. What is your return policy?

If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the item within 7 days of delivery for a refund or exchange. Items must be in their original packaging and unused condition.

5. How can I track my order?

After placing an order, you’ll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can also log in to your account and check the order status under “My Orders.”

6. What should I do if I have issues with my order?

If you face any issues, contact our customer support team through the “Contact Us” page or call us directly at +254-729-177-741. We’re here to help!
