Waliko Car Hire - Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a car? +
You can book a car online by selecting your preferred vehicle, choosing rental dates, and completing the booking form on our website.
Do I need a driving license? +
Yes, you need a valid driving license. International customers may use an international driving permit.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept M-Pesa, Visa, Mastercard, and PayPal for online bookings.
Is fuel included in the rental price? +
Fuel is not included. You must return the car with the same fuel level as when you picked it up.
Can I cancel my booking? +
Yes, you can cancel up to 24 hours before pickup without penalty. Later cancellations may incur charges.
Do you offer chauffeur services? +
Yes, we provide professional drivers at an additional cost. Please select the chauffeur option during booking.
Are there age restrictions for renting a car? +
Yes, you must be at least 21 years old with 1 year of driving experience to rent from us.
Do you provide roadside assistance? +
Yes, we offer 24/7 roadside assistance in case of breakdowns or emergencies during your rental period.
Can I extend my rental period? +
Yes, you can extend your rental by contacting us before your current contract ends. Additional charges apply.
What happens if I return the car late? +
Late returns may attract extra charges depending on how long past the agreed time the car is returned.
