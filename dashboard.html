Welcome to Waliko Car Hire

• This system allows you to view and book available cars.

• Click "Cars" to explore our fleet and press "Book Now" on any available vehicle.

• After booking, open "My Bookings" to see your pending reservations. You can delete a booking or proceed to make payment.

• Once payment is made, your status will be updated in the Payments section.

• To get a receipt, click the "Get Receipt" button inside the Payments section.

• If you experience a problem, press the "Help" button.

• By clicking "Settings", you can update your personal information, change your password, access Emergency Services, or manage account deletion/deactivation.
